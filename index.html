Intro

Here are the rules.

This is a emoji version of the classic board game Mastermind.

The computer will think of a secret code. The code will be the number of emojis chosen by you.

After your guess you get feedback:

A Red Peg: : Right color, Right Place

A White Peg: Right color, Wrong Place

Your job is to figure out that code within a certain amount of guesses. Are you ready to play?
